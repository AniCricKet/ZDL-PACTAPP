About Zero Degree Del Norte

At Zero Degree Del Norte, our mission is to contribute to the reduction of global temperature rise. We have developed the PACT app, a powerful tool designed to track individuals' greenhouse gas footprint and raise awareness about environmental impact.

The PACT app goes beyond simple calculations. It not only estimates user emissions but also evaluates the potential impact on children's health, emphasizing the urgency of taking steps towards a sustainable future. Our app empowers users by providing insights into their carbon footprint and encourages environmentally conscious decision-making.

Key Features of the PACT App:

  • Accurate calculation of user emissions
  • Assessment of the impact on children's health
  • Goal-setting functionality to help users reduce their carbon footprint

Join us in the journey towards a greener, healthier planet. Download the PACT app and start making a positive impact today!
